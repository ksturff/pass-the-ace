    HOW TO PLAY
    1
    Each player is dealt ONE CARD face down. Only you can see your own card.
    2
    On your turn, choose to KEEP your card or PASS it to the next player — forcing them to swap with you.
    3
    A KING cannot be passed — it blocks any swap. If the next player holds a King, your pass is blocked automatically.
    4
    At the end of each round, the player(s) holding the LOWEST CARD lose one chip.
    5
    Every player starts with chips. Lose all of them and you're eliminated. Last player standing wins!
    🪑 Sit & Stay — starts with 3 chips
    🛰️ Satellites — starts with 2 chips
    ⭐ Championship — starts with 2 chips
    ★
    Card values:
    A = 1 (lowest) 2–10 = face value J = 11 Q = 12 K = 13 (highest + blocks pass)
    GAME MODES
    🪑
    SIT & STAY — Quick cash games. Pay a chip buy-in, play a single 10-player table. Top 3 win chips. Starts with 3 chips. Games run continuously — join any time.
    🛰️
    SATELLITES — Free entry tournaments running every 3 hours (midnight, 3am, 6am … 9pm). Up to 500 players per satellite — split across multiple tables with the top players advancing to a Final Table. Top finishers earn a 🏆 Weekly Ticket. Starts with 2 chips.
    ⭐
    SATURDAY CHAMPIONSHIP — Requires a Weekly Ticket earned from a Satellite. Held every Saturday at 12pm, 3pm, 6pm, and 9pm. Win to earn a permanent 🏆 trophy on your profile. Starts with 2 chips.
    BATTLE PASS
    🌞
    SEASONAL BATTLE PASS — Each season (quarterly) has 12 weekly prizes. Complete all 3 of your daily tasks at least once during a week to unlock that week's reward — chips, cosmetics, and a Season Champion badge for finishing all 12 weeks. The first season is the Summer Pass, launching mid-June.
