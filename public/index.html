    HOW TO PLAY
    1
    Each player is dealt ONE CARD face down. Only you can see your own card.
    2
    On your turn, choose to KEEP your card or PASS it to the next player — forcing them to swap with you.
    3
    A KING cannot be passed — it blocks any swap. If the next player holds a King, your pass is blocked automatically.
    4
    At the end of each round, the player(s) holding the LOWEST CARD lose one life.
    5
    Every player starts with 3 LIVES. Lose all three and you're eliminated. Last player standing wins!
    ★
    Card values:
    A = 1 (lowest) 2–10 = face value J = 11 Q = 12 K = 13 (highest + blocks pass)
